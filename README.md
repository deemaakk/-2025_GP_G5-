# Introduction 
Laّweh is an innovative application designed to bridge the communication gap for individuals with hearing impairments, particularly those using Arabic Sign Language (ArSL). It serves as a two-way translation tool, converting ArSL into text and then speech as well as converting speech to text and then ArSL Letters.

By leveraging advanced AI technologies, Laّweh enhances accessibility, enabling seamless interactions between Hard of Hearning individuals and the wider community. The application aims to foster inclusivity, reduce communication barriers, and provide essential support in daily interactions, education, and professional environments.

# Launching Instructions for Laّweh
## For Sign to Text Translation:
1. Open the Laweh App
Launch the application on your device.

2. Allow Camera Access
Grant permission for the app to access your camera, enabling it to capture ArSL (Arabic Sign Language) letters.

3. Perform ArSL Letter
Position your hand in front of the camera, making the specific ArSL letter.

4. Text Display
The app will detect the letter and display the corresponding text on your screen in real-time.

5. Convert Text to Speech (Optional)
If desired, tap the option to convert the displayed text into speech, allowing for better accessibility.

6. Text Size Adjustment (Optional)
Optionally, adjust the text size for better readability based on your preference.

## For Text to Sign Translation:

1. Open the Application
Launch the application on your device and navigate to the text or speech input section.

2. Enter Text or Speak (Optional):

- Text Input: Type the text you want to convert into Arabic Sign Language.
- Speech Input (Optional): Speak into the microphone, and the system will convert your speech into text.
  
3. Convert to ArSL
The system will convert the text or speech input into animated Arabic Sign Language (ArSL) letters.

4. View ArSL Letter
The app will display the corresponding ArSL letter or animation on the screen for visual translation.
